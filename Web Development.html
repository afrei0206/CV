Avangard Omsk

Hockey Club.

Hockey Club Avangard is a Russian professional ice hockey team from Omsk.

Member of the Chernyshev Division in the Eastern Conference of the Kontinental Hockey League (KHL)

Champions

  • Gagarin Cup: 2021
  • Russian Superleague: 2004
  • KHL Continental Cup: 2011

Runners-up

  1. Gagarin Cup: 2012, 2019
  2. Russian Superleague: 2001, 2006
  3. IIHF Continental Cup: 2007
